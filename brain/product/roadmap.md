# Product Roadmap

## Purpose
This file tracks phased product delivery and strategic direction.

## How To Use
- Move items between phases as priorities change.
- Keep phases outcome-based rather than feature-dump lists.

## Phase 1: MVP
- Tenant setup and cooperative onboarding.
- Member registration and profile management.
- Member type capture for civil servant, individual, and business.
- Monthly contribution tracking.
- Configurable charge definitions.
- Monthly cooperative levy support.
- Loan request and approval workflow.
- Quick loan workflow with 3-month repayment.
- Normal loan workflow with 18-month repayment.
- Member transaction history and progress dashboard.
- Office operations dashboard and staff workflows.
- Admin visibility into pool status, loans, and defaulters.

## Phase 2: Trust And Operations
- Audit logs and stronger approval chains.
- Exportable statements and reports.
- Notifications for due payments and approvals.
- Basic KYC fields and document support.
- Offline mode with robust sync and reconciliation.
- Better delinquency handling and repayment collections.

## Phase 3: Financial Expansion
- Dividends workflow.
- External payment collection or bank integration.
- Advanced cooperative policy engine.
- Funding partner or liquidity support models.

## Strategic Risks To Watch
- Overcomplicating finance logic before the ledger is stable.
- Building disbursement flows before governance and permissions are solid.
- Promising universal 2x loans without pool-aware rules.
- Underestimating the complexity of offline sync for financial transactions.
